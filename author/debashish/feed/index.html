]]> The batmobile!

The concept car series continuously evolving to provide everything technology and imagination can afford, is perhaps the most widely admired and desired series of all time.

Well known for its tendency to “stay a step ahead of real life” at all times, the Batmobile has always represented the most exotic combination of defense machinery and sportiness whenever showcased to the eager and soon-to-be awestruck audience, irrespective of the era. Automobile fan or not, few can resist the sheer lust for power aroused by this ultimate ‘Badass’. 

As for Batman fans, already rejoicing at the release of the Dark Knight Rises, we are happy to announce that a documentary is to be  soon released this holiday season, revealing everything to do with the Batmobile. They can now look forward to a splendid gathering of some of the engineers, directors, actors, and drivers who made this fantasy killing machine real.
